Russian Verb Conjugations

This GUI provides a list of Russian verb conjugation types. They are designated by the ending of the verb that contains them. This GUI is used to select the conjugation type for the Russian verb being currently entered. To do so, click on the conjugation and then click "Accept." Note: It is possible to look up conjugations of Russian verbs on the Internet. In particular, try www.wiktionary.org.
