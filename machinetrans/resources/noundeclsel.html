Noun Declension Selection

This is the Noun declension selection screen. Here you select declensions for masculine, feminine, nueter and plural, animate and inanimate, and you do this once for singular and once for plural. The gender of the noun is changed by the declension you select. You choose the gender and number from the drop down up on top and then select the conjugation for that class by clicking on the list. Once again cancel will cancel this screen and accept will accept it.
