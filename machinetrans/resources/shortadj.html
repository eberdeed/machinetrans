Short Adjectives

Here you can edit the declensions of a short adjective. To edit the adjective either change each item in the line editors above or change the stem below and type "ENTER" that will update all the declensions above. And, of course, you are allowed to edit the English value. The back button will return you to the main adjective entry page. All edits made to this page and the previous page will be remembered by the system. Accepting the page will give you a listing of the SQL commands for these two pages and they will be entered into the database, unless of course you cancel.
