Miscellaneous Verb Conjugations

This GUI across the top has the line editors for the short form past passive participle, which you can edit to suit your needs. Note that if the verb has no past passive participle, there will be no short form and the editors will be empty. The stem of the short form is editable and typing "ENTER" after the edit will propagate the change throught the line editors. The short form English is also editable.

For the verbal adverbs and the imperatives you do not have to have values present. The program will automatically adjust for absent entries. Once again the English and Russian forms are editable. When this page is accepted the SQL review page is shown and any last minute corrections can be made then. Be careful not to break the SQL code or you will have to do the whole thing over.
